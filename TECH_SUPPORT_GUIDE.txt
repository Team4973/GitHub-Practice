ULTIMATE TECH SUPPORT TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
============================================

PROBLEM: Computer won't turn on
SOLUTION: Make sure it's plugged in. No, seriously. We've all been there.

PROBLEM: Internet not working
SOLUTION: Have you tried unplugging it and plugging it back in?
ALTERNATE SOLUTION: Blow on the ethernet cable like an old Nintendo cartridge

PROBLEM: Keyboard not working
SOLUTION: Check if you've been typing on your desk instead of the keyboard (happens more than you'd think)

PROBLEM: Mouse cursor moving on its own
SOLUTION: 
  1. Check for ghosts
  2. If no ghosts, check for cats
  3. If no cats, clean your mouse (the computer one)
  4. If still happening, accept your new AI overlord

PROBLEM: Screen is black
SOLUTION:
  - Is it turned on?
  - Is the brightness just really low?
  - Did you close your eyes?
  - Are you looking at the back of the monitor?

PROBLEM: Printer not working
SOLUTION: Printers are chaos incarnate. Throw it away. Buy a new one. It won't work either.
ADVANCED SOLUTION: Offer a small sacrifice to the printer gods (paper clips work)

PROBLEM: "No space left on device"
SOLUTION: Delete some of your 47,000 "untitled.txt" files

PROBLEM: WiFi password not working
SOLUTION:
  - Is Caps Lock on?
  - Are you typing the password written on the router or your neighbor's router?
  - Is it actually "Password123" and you just refuse to accept this reality?

PROBLEM: Computer running slow
SOLUTION:
  1. Close all 87 Chrome tabs
  2. Yes, even the ones from 2019
  3. Especially the ones from 2019

PROBLEM: Can't find a file
SOLUTION:
  - Check the Desktop
  - Check Downloads
  - Check Documents
  - Check the Recycle Bin
  - Cry
  - Find it 3 months later in a folder called "New Folder (7)"

PROBLEM: Video call not working
SOLUTION:
  - Unmute yourself (it's always this)
  - Turn on your camera
  - Remove the sticky note over your camera
  - Accept that your cat will walk across the keyboard at the worst moment

PROBLEM: "Error 404"
SOLUTION: The page doesn't exist. Move on with your life.

PROBLEM: "Error 500"
SOLUTION: Not your problem. Go get coffee.

PROBLEM: Program won't install
SOLUTION: Did you click "Next" like 500 times without reading anything? 
          You probably declined the EULA. Start over.

PROBLEM: Email won't send
SOLUTION:
  - Did you actually press send?
  - Are you connected to the internet?
  - Is it stuck in Outbox because you're pretending to work from a coffee shop?

PROBLEM: Nothing works and you've tried everything
SOLUTION: Restart your computer. This fixes 94% of all problems.
LAST RESORT: Is Mercury in retrograde?
